Consent to Receive SMS Messages

By using the MaidSmart platform, you consent to receive operational SMS messages from MaidSmart LLC related to facility management alerts and issue resolution.

SMS consent is obtained when an authorized facility manager registers your phone number in the MaidSmart system, or when you scan a MaidSmart QR code and provide your phone number.

Message Types Operational alerts about reported facility issues and resolution confirmations
Frequency Messages are event-driven, sent only when an issue is reported at your assigned facility
Costs Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan
No Marketing MaidSmart will never send promotional or marketing messages via SMS

How MaidSmart SMS Works

MaidSmart is a facility management platform. When a visitor at a facility scans a QR code and reports an issue (such as a restroom needing attention), the assigned EVS (Environmental Services) worker receives an SMS alert. The worker can reply to confirm the issue has been resolved.

Example message:

Feedback: Room 1011 was marked as clean up needed. Please reply with Y once it is completed.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any MaidSmart text message. You will receive a one-time confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

You may also contact your facility manager to have your number removed from the system, or email us at help@maidsmart.app to request removal.

Privacy

MaidSmart LLC respects your privacy. Phone numbers collected through the MaidSmart platform are used solely for sending operational facility management alerts. We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your information is handled in accordance with our privacy practices. For questions, contact help@maidsmart.app.
